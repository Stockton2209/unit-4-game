The Mystical Crystal Counting Game

The game begins with a randomly-generated target number.

The four crystals each have a different value. Click on them to add to your total score.

Win the game by matching your score to the target number. If your score exceeds the target number, I'm afraid you'll lose and will have to try again.

The value of the crystals is hidden and changes with each new game!
